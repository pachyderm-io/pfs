Pipeline Concepts
=================

Pachyderm Pipeline System (PPS) is a computational
component of the Pachyderm platform that enables you to
perform various operations with your data, including
creating and modifying your data pipelines, breaking up
your data to smaller processing chunks called datums for
optimized performance, combine data from various
repositories and branches to mix and match the inputs in
different combinations.
